Weekly Calendar

What To Expect

No need to get changed, stay in your school uniform.

Recommend you bring a packed lunch with you, as this will be quicker than going to the canteen for food. Eat your lunch at archery.

There are a large number of people who are new to the sport. The first session will be slow as we will need to go through safety rules and how to shoot. The following week will go much quicker, and you will be doing far more shooting.
